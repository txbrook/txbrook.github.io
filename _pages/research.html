Research Interests

My research interests focus on efficient reasoning and causal inference, with an emphasis on building scalable and reliable machine learning systems. In particular, my work emphasizes:

  • Efficient reasoning
  • Causal inference
  • Multimodal representation learning
  • Optimization
  • The design of MCP and multi-turn interaction frameworks for general-purpose applications
